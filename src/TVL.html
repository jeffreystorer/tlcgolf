GRAB-&-GO


The Village Library is open with curbside service only.

The Details!

1. We are open Wednesday through Saturday.

2. Our catalog is now available online. Click here to view our catalog

3. Call 598-1183 between 10AM and 1PM to reserve your books. Please limit your selections to 3 adult books or 5 children's books.

4. Curbside pickup is between 1PM and 2:30PM. Please stay in your car, pop your trunk, and a volunteer will bring your books to you.

5. We do not accept returns. You must still return books using the library drop box.

6. If your membership needs to be renewed or fines are due, we will let you know when you pick your books up and what the options are to pay.

Buy A Brick Program

The Buy A Brick Program is a means of raising funds to support the library. The bricks border the entry to the library. In addition to supporting the library by having bricks engraved with their own names, our donors have had bricks engraved with the names of their grandchildren, to commemorate the passing of a loved one, or to honor a friend.

Brick donations are $200 each. All contributions are tax -deductible as the library is a not-for-profit organization. Brick donor forms are available at the library front desk. Checks should be made payable to The Village Library.
